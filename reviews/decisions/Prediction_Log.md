## Example Entry

### Date
2026-03-02

### Theme
AI Infrastructure Buildout Is Durable

### Thesis Health at Entry
8

### Decision Type
Watch only

### Prediction
AI infrastructure demand will remain strong even if model leadership rotates, because power, networking, and optics remain bottlenecks independent of any single model winner.

### Time Horizon
4 to 6 quarters

### Confidence
Medium

### Why I Believe It
- Physical infrastructure bottlenecks are harder to solve quickly than software bottlenecks
- The value chain is broader than any one lab or model provider
- Demand should show up across multiple sub-sectors if the thesis is real

### Best Expressions
- VRT
- ANET
- ETN

### Why This Name Instead of Another Expression
- VRT and ANET are cleaner direct beneficiaries than broader market proxies
- ETN provides a more durable but less pure exposure than some smaller names

### Main Risks / Counter-Narrative
- AI demand temporarily digests after overbuild
- Efficiency gains reduce urgency of new infrastructure investment
- The market has already priced in too much durability too early

### What Would Prove Me Wrong
- Two or more direct-exposure names guide down on AI demand in the same cycle
- Networking and optics vendors guide down together
- Power and cooling lead times normalize quickly and backlog rolls over

### Valuation / Crowding Note
- A strong thesis can still be a bad entry if the market is already discounting multiple years of perfection
